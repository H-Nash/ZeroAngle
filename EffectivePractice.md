# Effective Practice Guideline
## 4 Levels of Practice
- Theory
- Dry Running
- Scrim
- Review

## Theory
- Theory crafting is essential
- Theory crafting doesn't mean JUST the IGL
- Try to think outside of the box
- Use you resources(nartouthere, tiktok/youtube, pro demo review, etc)
- Develop Protocols for different scenarios (if the other team is playing retake, if they have taken space somewhere, how you want to retake a site.)
- When developing strats figure out how to taylor it to your team and make changes to suit your team.

## Dry running
- Use resources (pracc mod, practicemode mod, refrag)
- Make it game like ( if you have a coach, have them use utility so that your protocols stay intact)
- Do it until it feels comfortable
- Re-Dry run old tactics when you haven't used them in a while.

## Scrim
- Two types of scrimming (scrimming to win/ goal based scrimming)
  - Scrimming to win (40%) (Not By Any Means Necessary)
    - Keeps you prepared for matches, this is good to do before officials.
    - Allows IGL to call a more fluid style.
    - Allows players to feel more comfortable making plays
  - Goal based scrimming (60%)
    - Set specific goals & meet them!
    - if you need to work on executes, try to get your team in position for that execute practically, and then use them. (Dont just do it out of spawn)
    - If you know you've been struggling on defaults, default all scim.
    - try to use few things, rather than everything. (Get the reps in on the things you need to focus on)

## Review
- Use your resources (pracc.com, refrag pov locked demos, live streaming to unlisted youtube, recording povs through OBS or other recorders, or record actual pov demos in game.)
- Review constantly & then use that review to curve your theory crafting as well as you scrim focus (ie scrim to win more, goal based scrimming more)
- Critique! This is the best time to critique as it comes from a neutral standpoint. No one likes being told they messed up in the moment, but in a neutral setting, most can find common ground.
