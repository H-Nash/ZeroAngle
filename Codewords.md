# Code Words
- Joker  - Someone is in a good position make a play or backstab and needs some time.
- Jester - Someone is offering to be bait for another player.
- Sound - Cut coms and let that person listen.
- Cancel - Whatever play was called you will "cancel" it and regroup.
- Double - Asking someone to double swing an angle with you right now.
- 123 - asking someone to double swing on the count of 3.
- Freeze - everyone should stop taking space and or fights.
