# Defaults
Defaults - The default way your team will position each round.
T-Side - Getting map control for one important part of the map while controling and getting info for other parts of the map.
CT-Side - Getting map control for one part of the map while holding for lurks and getting infor around the map.
## Ancient
You can either fight for cheeta or mid control. 2 elbow and 3 b will be the setup for both of these cases.
### T-Side
On B side 2 players are shelf 1 player is ramp. Shelf players are either challenging for cheeta control or challenging for mid control. Ramp player holds a push or mollies pillar to help take cave.
Mid players are denying CT players access to jumpup with utility. they then can lurk through elbow smoke, nade through, or wait for shelf players.
### CT-Side
B anchor, incharge of ramp util to stop a rush. 
Jaguar player that throws shelf utility.
2 mid riflers throwing a molly and 2 nades to deny midrush. 
A awper who should be throwing instant elbowsmoke for mid players. They can kinda go where ever they want.
## Dust 2
Heavily spawn dependant
### T-Side
2 players tunnels, 1 holding upper and 1 holding lower early. They can take lowertogether or let lower hold take it solo.
1 player drops suicide that can spot mid for lower or mid push and they can throw mid smoke and short molly if they are taking mid.
2 players taking top mid. 1 can become long lurk or they can let suicide player become long lurk after throwing utility.
Goal is to take mid and take short to pressure both mid B take or short take.
### CT-Side
1 B anchor who will use most of their util to block of upper tuns early.
1 Mid player who's whole job is to gather info and maybe do some chip damage. If it is a low buy for the T's they should be double B early to stop a rush.
2 A players normally with good spawns taking long or cat control. 
1 A rotate who throws supporting utility for either take and then rotates to pick up mid.

## Inferno
You want to fight for car and bracket control.
### T-Side
**Banana Control**
2 banana players get into banan early and work together to take cars.
1 mid player who will stop a mid rush and help take banana with utility if needed.
1 Boiler player coming through altmid to get into boiler. they take aparement with Apps lurk.
Apps lurker will come from altmid taking apps from t steps working with boiler player to take full apps control.
### CT-Side
Setup is very flexible.
2 Car players that work together to take banana control.
1 Arches "awper" who can start at b if needed.
1 Short player who is holding boiler.
1 Pit player who is holding apps lurk.

## Mirage
### T-Side
A lurk that plays in ramp or palace, they are just holding for pushes early and activeates when they can pinch.
3 mid players taking mid control with top mid and window smoke. They are attempting to split either A through Con or B from Cat Or get a lurk to connector Window or ladder. Then the others regroup hitting a site together with lurk in their position.
1 B/underpass lurker they can hold apps if the plan is to go b late or they can begin to push underpass once it is safe to do so.
### CT-Side
1 A anchor who supports mid fight with early mid flash.
1 Con player who mollies ramp and helps fight middle.
1 Window player "awper" attempts to get early picks then rotates to support one of the other sites.
1 Cat player who helps fight mid early if that is the plan.
1 B anchor jump spotting for info in apps.
